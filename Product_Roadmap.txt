🟠 validate product assumptions early
🟡 unlock second major use cases
🟢 build moat & defensibility
🔵 expand market reach
🟣 prepare for monetization

Status: Already in progress
✔ Gmail connection
✔ Email scanning
✔ Rules system
✔ Digest creation

Remaining Deliverables

✔ Intent + importance classification

POSITIONING & WHY IT WILL WORK

Superhuman: fast email client
MailOS: smart email understan ding + action layer

MailOS helps users who:
✔ don’t want to manage email
✔ want to reduce cognitive load
✔ miss important context
✔ want summaries not friction
✔ want email to serve their life, not interrupt it

WHO WOULD PAY?

Your potential paying users include:

🔹 Freelancers who need information clarity
🔹 Job seekers needing pipeline tracking
🔹 Professionals with heavy newsletters
🔹 Founders who need inbox summaries
🔹 Finance-savvy people
🔹 Teams who share inbox responsibilities

You are building a utility platform, not a niche gimmick.
